About ScamShield UK

ScamShield UK is an independent, free community project. We are NOT a UK government website and NOT affiliated with the Singapore Government's ScamShield app (scamshield.gov.sg).

What is ScamShield UK?

ScamShield UK (scamshield.org.uk) is a free AI-powered tool that helps people in the United Kingdom check suspicious messages, emails, phone numbers, and websites for signs of fraud. It was created to give everyday people a quick, plain-English way to find out if something is a scam.

The tool analyses messages against 50+ known fraud patterns, provides instant threat intelligence reports, and directs users to the correct official UK reporting channels.

What We Are NOT

To be completely transparent:

  • We are not a government website — not UK, not Singapore, not any government
  • We are not affiliated with Action Fraud, the FCA, the police, or any official body
  • We are not the Singapore ScamShield (scamshield.gov.sg) — that is a separate government initiative
  • We do not collect personal data, passwords, or bank details
  • We do not store messages — all analysis happens in real time and is deleted immediately

Why Trust Us?

  • Completely free — no paid tiers, no premium features, no upselling
  • No account or registration required
  • No personal data collected or stored

  • Open about our independent status

  • Privacy-first design — essential cookies only

Official UK Scam Reporting Channels

We are not a replacement for official reporting. If you have been scammed, please contact these official organisations directly:

  • Action Fraud — actionfraud.police.uk — National fraud and cybercrime reporting (call 0300 123 2040)
  • FCA — fca.org.uk — Report financial scams and check the FCA Register
  • ICO — ico.org.uk — Report data breaches and privacy concerns
  • Ofcom — ofcom.org.uk — Report nuisance calls and texts
  • Citizens Advice — citizensadvice.org.uk — Free consumer advice
  • In an emergency, call 999
