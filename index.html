Our Mission

Protecting the UK from Scams & Fraud

ScamShield UK is a free, independent tool built to help ordinary people spot, avoid, and report fraud. We believe scam protection should be available to everyone.

  • ✓ No personal data stored — ever
  • ✓ No account, no login, no subscription
  • ✓ Directs users to official UK reporting channels
  • ✓ Completely free, always
Important: ScamShield UK (scamshield.org.uk) is an independent community tool. It is not a UK government website and is not affiliated with the Singapore Government ScamShield app. We do not collect passwords, bank details, or personal data. Always report fraud to Action Fraud.
